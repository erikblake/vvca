Welcome to the website of the Valleyview Community Association

  • Valleyview is located in Whitehorse, Yukon, Canada, the Wilderness City.
  • The Valleyview Community Association provides representation for Valleyview residents, develops and strengthens the welfare of the Valleyview Community, and promotes and coordinates social and recreational activities for Valleyview Residents.

If you have questions or concerns, please contact one of our board members, or visit our Facebook page.
